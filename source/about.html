About

Spearheaded by it’s creative director and entrepreneur, Claudio Mendonca, CCM Design has over twelve years of experience designing brand identities, data visualizations, online experiences and digital products.

Design is our passion and we do it with excellence. We are always challenging our creativity to deliver the very best products for our clients. Today, CCM Design’s work is a design reference for civic tech, working with those who make the difference, we feel proud of the place we occupy in this scenario.

Our deep knowledge of branding and graphic design is gracefully coupled with the ever evolving fields of User Experience and Web Development, in an unique interdisciplinary team of great people and talented professionals. We work with our clients on a broader branding vision, including editorial design, interfaces, user experience, videos and more.

Welcome to CCM Design website! We hope you find inspiration here. If you’d like to get in touch with us, we’ll be glad to hear from you.
